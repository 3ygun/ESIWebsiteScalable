Hello, my name is David Soller. I'm an aspiring computer scientist attempting to make my impact on the world through software development. Software development from my prospective is the perfect combination of design, creation, and collaboration. The element of design effects backend creation as well as the final user product. Evident throughout the software in simplistic but meaningful ways such as button placement and content portrayal. Creation is at the developmental core. Creation is the job, and is used to accomplish it. Finally, software development is based around collaboration. We take in building blocks, rearrange & reorder them, in order to design and create a finished product.
