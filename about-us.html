About the eSports Initiative

Our Mission

eSports Initiative works to bring together recreational and competitive video game players, as well as the digital media industry, to raise money for local and national causes. We work to beneficially impact the community while maintaining a positive image of the industry and its consumers. More directly, we hold video game events and tournaments to raise funds for charity and we volunteer in the local Columbus community all while improving the Midwest eSports scene.

Description: Current goals

The eSports Initiative are to empower local Columbus-area students to study STEM (science, technology, engineering, and math)-related fields, and to build a giving community with gaming as its focal point.

Who makes ESI?

The eSports Initiative is made up of Faculty, past graduates, and Ohio State University students.
